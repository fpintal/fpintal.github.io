Project Overview | YtheTech Nail Booking

The YtheTech Nail Booking website allows clients to browse services, view nail art tiers, and schedule appointments online. It provides a simple and organized way for customers to book appointments while viewing service details and examples of previous work.

Intended Users: Nail clients booking appointments with YtheTech at Pure Nails on 55.

Content Overview: Home, Services, Art Tiers, Gallery, Booking, Contact, and Policies pages.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Flatpickr date picker for selecting appointment dates.
  • Interactive booking form with JavaScript validation.
  • Lightbox gallery for nail art images.
  • Reusable header and footer across all pages.

Page Designs

Each page includes consistent navigation, pink theme styling, and interactive features.

  • Home: Overview of services, art tiers, gallery preview, and booking CTA.
  • Services & Pricing: Full list of nail services and add-ons.
  • Art Tiers: Four-tier structure with descriptions and sample styles.
  • Gallery: Work image grid with lightbox pop-up view.
  • Booking: Appointment form with date picker and upload option.
  • Confirmation: Thank-you page summarizing booked appointment.
  • Contact: Location, hours, and contact information.
  • Policies & FAQs: Deposit, cancellation, and client prep info.
